Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

Is my data secure? ▼
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for all data. Plus, our self-hosted option lets you keep 100% control of your data on your own server.
How accurate are the FIFO calculations? ▼
Our FIFO calculations are precise to 8 decimal places and use exact transaction prices (not rounded values) to ensure 100% accuracy for tax reporting.
Which exchanges and brokers do you support? ▼
We support CSV imports from any exchange or broker including Revolut, Bitpanda, Coinbase, Kraken, Binance, Interactive Brokers, and many more. If it exports CSV, we can import it.
Can I track multiple currencies? ▼
Absolutely! We natively support multiple currencies including EUR, USD, and more. Each currency is tracked separately with its own performance metrics.
What's the difference between cloud and self-hosted? ▼
Cloud (Pro plan) is hosted by us - just sign up and start using. Self-hosted means you download and run the software on your own server for maximum privacy and control.
Do you offer a free trial? ▼
Yes! You can start with our Free plan (up to 100 transactions) or try Pro with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
